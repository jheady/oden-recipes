Macaroni and Cheese

This recipe makes delicious macaroni and cheese.

It's cheap and easy, great for a student's budget.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • uncooked elbow macarnoi pasta
  • butter
  • milk
  • Velveeta cheese
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Toss pinch of salt into water
  3. Add uncooked pasta to boiling water
  4. Once pasta is tender, drain water
  5. Return drained pasta to pan
  6. Add milk, cheese, and butter to pasta
  7. Stir until fully mixed
  8. Serve and enjoy
